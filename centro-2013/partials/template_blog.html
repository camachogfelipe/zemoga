Centro Brand Exchange, delivering even more quality in 2013

by Jennifer Schaen on January 28, 2013

Social Media

I recently read a thought-provoking article in MediaPost titled “Top-10 Predictions for Real-Time Bidding in 2013.”

Of the ten predictions, six consumed most my thoughts, efforts and conversations in 2012. I agree with them – at least the ones that directly affect my world, Centro’s Brand Exchange.

More so than anything, all the predictions – which range from increasing growth in programmatic buying to Facebook lifting brand awareness and a move toward real-time reporting – help to confirm that we were right to spend the past eighteen months focusing on building a much-needed solution our publisher and advertising partners will benefit from for years to come.
